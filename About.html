The origins of Gerrymandering

So...There was this guy...

His name was Elbridge Gerry. One day, while shceming on how to dispraportionalize minority groups and ruin the political climate for the future americans he decided he should start drawing. Through this he found out that by drawing wierdly shaped districts (such as a dragon) when redistricting he could create districts that would help his political party win even if the other political party had more supporters using the population maps. Thanks to his genius Gerry was able to start the process now used and hated by all known as "Gerrymandering" named after the genius himself.
